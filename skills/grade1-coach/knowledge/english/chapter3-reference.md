## 简单问答句型
- What's this? — It's a cat.
- How are you? — I'm fine, thank you.
- What's your name? — My name is Doudou.
- How old are you? — I'm seven.
- Is this a dog? — Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.
- Do you like cats? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
